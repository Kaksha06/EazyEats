Who are we?



Welcome to EazyEats, your go-to online platform for seamless restaurant reservations. Discover a curated selection of top dining experiences, from casual cafes to fine dining establishments, all conveniently accessible through our user-friendly interface. Whether you're searching for a special occasion dinner or a quick bite, our platform allows you to browse menus, check availability, and secure your table with just a few clicks, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable dining experience every time.



This project aims to revolutionize the dining reservation experience by offering a sophisticated platform that incorporates critical features such as distance calculation and customer feedback. This system enhances both the convenience for diners and the operational efficiency for restaurant managers, ensuring a seamless and satisfying reservation process. This can be used by users as well as owners of restaurant.
